Christoph Burgdorf — Using AngularJS for your regular website

At CouchCommerce Christoph helped to replace their old website with a new shiny AngularJS powered replacement. In his talk Christoph will shed light on the question if AngularJS is a good fit for regular websites or not. He will answer how they managed to built the website within 3 weeks considering that the two main actors had no experience with AngularJS before at all.

What also will be covered is how they tackled the challenges with SEO that come along with a client side architecture.

If you are in doubt that AngularJS is a good fit for your regular website - this talk is for you!
